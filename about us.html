Our Story

The Fix is a South African fashion retailer under The Foschini Group (TFG). We focus on trendy, youth-inspired, and affordable fashion for everyone.

Our Vision

To create the most remarkable omnichannel experiences for our customers.

Our Mission

To give all South Africans access to a relevant and inspiring fashion lifestyle, irrespective of identity, body type, style, budget, where they live or how busy their lives are.

Our Values

  • Trendy
  • Affordable
  • Inclusive clothing
